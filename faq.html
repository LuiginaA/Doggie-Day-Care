Frequently Asked Questions

Wondering What Questions You Should Ask?

  • ♦ What is included for the rate?
      • Time will be spent by social interaction, walks, stimulating play, as well as free playtime.
  • ♦ Potty Training:
      • Should you be in the middle of the potty training period we will help by continuing with your program.
  • ♦ Special Needs or Requirements:
      • Any special needs or food requirements must be discussed before hand so that we can make the necessary arrangements to accommodate this for you.
  • ♦ Will my fur baby be locked up all day?
      • No definitely not! The only time they are separated is when they need to eat, this is for those babies that have special needs due to special health conditions or age related issues.
  • ♦ What are your facilities like?
      • Our facilities are large and even when it is necessary to confine a doggie, the allocted area is large enough for them to play and run around and get the necessary exercise although they will also take part in our daily programme.
  • ♦ Can the premises be viewed before hand?
      • Yes of course! We take pride in our facilities which is kept in pristine condition at all times. You are also more than welcome to meet our staff so that your mind is at ease.
  • ♦ What is the procedure in case of an emergency or should any doggie take ill suddenly?
      • You will be notified immediately! Sick doggies will be confined to an allocated crib in order to contain any risk of infection. Depending on the severity of the emergency, the on-call emergency vet will be called in to examine your baby.
  • ♦ Do you have; Rules, regulations and guidelines that are to be followed?
      • Yes, in order for us to provide you with our best service, rules need to be in place and adhered to. These documents can be given to you on request. When you register your fur baby for day care you will need to complete this documents.
  • ♦ Vaccinations requirements?
      • Before being able to sign up for day care, all necessary vaccinations must be in order. A copy of the record must be given together with the completed documentation to our receptionist on application.
