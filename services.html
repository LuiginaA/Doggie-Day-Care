Our Services!

What we Offer!

Do you work all day? Are you finding that your precious bundle of fur is feeling lonely, uncared for, suffers from separation, anxiety, has issues around boredom or needs regular feeding. Then why not consider leaving your fur baby in our care, allowing you peace of mind while at work.

As a family owned business, we are a team of caring and dedicated dog handlers. We take pride in giving your dog a comfortable loving home away from home environment.

We do our best to alleviate any anxiety and stress that can negatively impact any animal, including physical and psychological changes. This is done by introducing and integrating new members with care and caution allowing for time to adjust in a new environment.

Daily Activities

A day spent with our team at our facilities is filled with enriched activities and lots of play. Some other activities also included are as follows:

  • Lots of love and hugs.
  • Feeding, when required.
  • Socialisation and integration with the other fur babies.
  • Individual group times to separate age, size and experience for certain activities.
  • A variety of games such as tug of war with various toys, sandpit, jungle gym and tunnel play.

Special Extras!

  • Our own homemade treats are not only tasty and healthy but rewarding after any basic training, or good deed that has successfully been carried out during basic obedience and training.
  • Comfortable beds, chairs or cushions to relax and sleep on while calm music plays in the background. Each baby will receive a massage and brush during this time.
  • An update on how your baby is coping in their new environment while you are at work. This is done with short videos or photos. Should you request a more detailed report, one can be given to you verbally on collection of your fur baby.
  • A free of charge shampoo and brush will be offered for your fur baby as a regular at our centre.
