About Us

Looking for a loving environment for your fur baby. Look no further! Doggie Day Care is a home away from home.

Doggie Day Care is a family run business which has been open for almost 3 years. We specialise in dogs only in order to give them the attention they so deserve. A dog is more than just a pet; they're an extended family member.

We are a team of dedicated, caring, dog handlers. Our 90% regular attendance has been with us since we opened and is growing daily.

Our "New" members are introduced and integrated with care and caution allowing for time to adjust in their new environment. All efforts are taken to prevent your doggie from suffering any stress or anxiety. Our aim is to provide a safe and loving home away from home.

Our routines vary daily from walks to stimulating playtime as well as social interaction with other doggies. We also initiate basic training command techniques, eg sit, walk, stay, etc.

Our Facilities

Here at our centre we do not lock up your fur baby in a crib unless specific requirements are requested by the owner personally.

Our property facilitates large enclosures with loads of space for your fur baby to stretch. our facilities are cleaned and sanitised daily. We do our best to ensure that all requirements are met without making your baby feel stressed due to being locked up in a tiny enclosure.
