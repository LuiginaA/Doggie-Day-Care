We are just a "WAG" away!

Do you feel anxious or even sad when you have to leave your fur baby to go to the office or to visit a friend, knowing that they will be left on their own to their own devices for hours on end?

You know they will get up to mischief because of boredom or loneliness. They are just as anxious and stressed as you are. They too suffer from abandonment leaving them feeling kind of lost and alone, not knowing when you will return.

Do you perhaps have a fur baby that needs that extra attention because of health reasons and cannot be left all day.

Well we have the solution to help you and your fur baby. Our day care centre will give you peace of mind and most of all it will be a home away from home in a loving and safe environment for your loyal friend and member of your family.

Here at Doggie Day Care we offer the best possible care and mental and physical stimulation through play, socialising and interactive activities with fully trained dog handlers. We ensure that your fur baby will get all the comfort and attention they need.

You will be delighted and content knowing your baby is well taken care of, an experience with the joy and enthusiasm to return.
